Meet our Team

WE are an interdisciplinary team working to push the boundaries of construction cyberTraining, BIM CI, and cognitive science from Michigan Technological University and Western University. Led by the PI Bo Xiao, our team has three faculty members and six graduate students are trained through this project. An estimated advisory board has been established to provide constructive advice and ensure the success of this project.
