GenAI CyberTraining Platform for Construction Education

Project Information
Supported by National Science Foundation (NSF), this project aims to develop an  interactive CyberTraining platform powered by cognitive and generative AI to advance construction education with a focus onBuilding Information Modeling (BIM) Cyber Infrastructure (CI). The proposed CyberTraining platform provides an interactive, customized, and course-oriented learning environment by knowledge space theories, scaffold learning, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). A four-week BIM learning module has been developed and integrated into the platform.

Intellectual Merit

This project reimagines BIM CI training by combining cognitive science with generative technology to deliver scalable, personalized, and hands-on learning in construction education.

  • Personalized Training: Filling the gap of CI CyberTraining in construction
  • Flexible Content: Generative design for diversify BIM Training materials
  • Scalable Platform: Easily to be extended to other STEM education fields

Broader Impacts

This project enhances BIM CI education and strengthens the skilled workforce pipeline through personalized, accessible training across all levels.

  • Construction Education: Advance students' skill sets in BIM CI
  • Widely CI Training: Lead the genAI transformation of the educational ecosystem
  • Society: Mitigate the nation's shortage of skilled construction workers
